ABOUT THE HACKATHON

Welcome to BUILDATHON 4.0 - ITM's flagship hackathon event! This is a 24-hour coding marathon where innovation meets creativity. Form teams, tackle real-world challenges, and build solutions that matter.

Whether you're a coding wizard or just starting your journey, Buildathon offers the perfect platform to showcase your skills, learn from peers, and win exciting prizes!

May 30-31, 2025
ITM Campus, Kharghar
Team Size: 2-4 Members
